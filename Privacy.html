Also, the user contact number that is asked while signing in, is solely for the purpose of logging in to the application. We assure you that your contact information of any sort will not be used by us for any other purposes

EmaChat uses third-party services but will still have no access to your personal information. your data is neither stored with us or any of the third-party services that we acquire. We respect your privacy and handle it with utmost discretion. The third-party servers would use only user information like browser information and user-agent. Other data collection and usage information that you must know:

1. Log Data : We want to inform you that whenever you use our service, in case of an error in the app we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called log data. This log data mostly includes the operating system version of the phone and no other information is collected. This information, after the issue is resolved is automatically cleared out of the system

These are sent to your browser from the websites you visit and are stored in the internal memory of your device. We do not store any of your data through these cookies. This service explicitly uses cookies, to enhance the user interface. When you choose to avail our services, you automatically accept these cookies too.

Security

We value your trust in providing us your information, so it is our promise that we will protect it too. The chats and photos will be end-user-encrypted with AES 256 Bit Encryption with 16 Cipher and all the conversations will be deleted immediately from the server as soon as the message is delivered and after the chat session has ended, it will be deleted from the user's device too.
